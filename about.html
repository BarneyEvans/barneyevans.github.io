My Teaching Approach

How I Teach

I offer a flexible, technology-enhanced learning experience:

  • Remote Learning: Lessons are conducted remotely via Microsoft Teams, with in-person options available exclusively within the Southampton area.
  • Recorded Sessions: All remote sessions are recorded (with permission) and made available to parents for review, allowing them to stay involved with their child's progress.
  • Interactive Learning: My lessons feature real-time document collaboration, allowing students to actively participate rather than passively watch. This hands-on approach leads to better understanding and retention.
  • Privacy Assured: All recordings are kept secure and shared only with parents/guardians, adhering to strict data protection principles.

Regular assessments help us identify strengths and areas for improvement, allowing us to adjust our approach for optimal results.
